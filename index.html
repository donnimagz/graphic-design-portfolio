About Don

I'm an accomplished, multidisciplinary graphic designer and art director with over 8 years of experience, based in Kampala, Uganda. I bring creativity, versatility, and strategic thinking to every project — with a passion for visual storytelling and a keen eye for detail.

I conceptualise and execute innovative ideas that deliver powerful visual impact, translating complex concepts into visually captivating designs. I'm also a Sickle Cell Warrior & Advocate, and co-founder of the Raremark Foundation — a global community for people affected by Sickle Cell Disease.
